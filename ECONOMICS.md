# Unit Economics

## 💰 Converted Lead Value to Credex
An average startup lead (Series A, 50 seats) switching their AI spend (Cursor, Claude, Copilot) to Credex is worth:
- **Total Annual AI Spend:** ~$25,000
- **Credex Margin (Estimated):** 10% (after sourcing discounted credits)
- **Gross Profit Per Lead:** $2,500/year
- **LTV (2-year retention):** **$5,000**

## 📉 CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
Based on the GTM plan ($0 paid budget, founder time only):
- **Content/Social:** $0 (Opportunity cost: 10 hours/week = ~$1,000)
- **Hosting/API Costs:** ~$0.05 per audit (Gemini Flash + Vercel free tier)
- **Blended CAC:** ~$10 per qualified lead (if we get 100 leads from 10 hours of work).

## 📊 Conversion Math (The Profitability Path)
To make this tool a massive success:
1.  **Audits Completed:** 1,000
2.  **Email Lead Capture (15%):** 150 leads
3.  **Credex Consultation Booked (10% of leads):** 15 bookings
4.  **Credit Purchase Closed (30% of bookings):** 5 customers
- **Total Revenue (5 × $2,500):** $12,500
- **Total Tool OpEx:** $50 (API costs for 1k audits)
- **ROI:** **250x**

## 🚀 The Path to $1M ARR
To drive $1M in Annual Recurring Revenue for Credex in 18 months, the tool needs to:
- **Process 10,000 audits per month.**
- **Average $10,000/year spend per closed customer.**
- **Close 100 customers.**
- **Requires a 1% "Audit -> Customer" conversion rate.**
This is highly achievable if the tool becomes the "de facto" benchmark for AI spend in the startup ecosystem.

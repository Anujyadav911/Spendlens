## FAQ
**1. Is this tool actually free?**
Yes. SpendLens is a free lead-generation asset for Credex. We provide the audit for free because we believe once you see your savings opportunities, you'll want to capture them through Credex.

**2. Do you need my credit card or bank login?**
Never. This is a "manual-entry" audit. You just tell us what plans you're on and how many seats you have. We don't need access to your billing data.

**3. Is my audit data public?**
By default, audits are private and stored locally. If you choose to generate a "Shareable Link," we strip all identifying details (company name, email) from the public version of the report.

**4. How accurate is the pricing?**
Our engine uses live-verified pricing data for Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT, and 5 other major tools. We verify our sources weekly to ensure accuracy.

**5. How does Credex make money?**
Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. If your audit shows a high savings opportunity (> $500/mo), we'll offer you a consultation to move your spend to our discounted marketplace.
